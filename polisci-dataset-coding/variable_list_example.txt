

### **D1: Clergy/Ulama Political Marginalization**

*Captures state restrictions on religious scholars' political participation and the dismantling of clerical institutions.*

| Variable | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `clergy_institution_abolish` | Were traditional clerical institutions abolished or dismantled? |
| `clergy_appointment` | Does the state control appointment of senior religious figures? |
| `fatwa_authority` | What is the legal standing of religious rulings (fatwas)? |
| `religious_party_ban` | Are religiously-based political parties banned? |
| `clergy_office_ban` | Are clergy banned from holding elected/appointed political office? |
| `clergy_party_ban` | Are clergy banned from political party membership or leadership? |
| `clergy_speech_ban` | Are clergy banned from political speech, endorsements, or commentary? |
| `clergy_dress_ban` | Are clergy required to secularize their dress/appearance in public? |
| `clergy_licensing` | Must clergy be licensed/registered by the state to preach or lead prayers? |
| `mosque_political_ban` | Are political activities banned in mosques? |
| `sermon_regulation` | Does state regulate or approve sermon content? |
| `religious_org_political` | Can religious organizations engage in political advocacy? |
| `minority_clergy_restrict` | Do minority sect clergy face additional restrictions beyond those applied to majority? |
| `clergy_state_power` | **Flag:** Do clergy hold formal state power? (Used to identify religious states). |
